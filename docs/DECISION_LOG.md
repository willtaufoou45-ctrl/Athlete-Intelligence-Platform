## Decision #1

**Date:** June 28, 2026

**Topic:** Product Workflow

### Decision

The primary coaching workflow is organized around **2–3 week training blocks**, not individual training sessions.

### Why

Coaches do not redesign training every day.

They create a training block with a specific objective and evaluate progress through objective testing every 2–3 weeks.

The software should reinforce the current plan rather than interrupt it.

### Product Impact

The coach dashboard should immediately show:

* Current training block
* Block objective
* Primary coaching cues
* Athlete response (Improving / Plateau / Regressing)
* Next evaluation date
* Recommendation: Stay the course or adjust

### Guiding Principle

AIP should never tell a great coach how to coach.

It should remind coaches what they have already decided, organize athlete information around the current training block, and provide objective evidence for when it is time to adjust.
