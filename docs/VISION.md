# Athlete Intelligence Platform

## Mission

Objective testing is the compass that guides coaching decisions with confidence.

We help coaches make better decisions through objective testing that leads to measurable athlete improvement.

---

## Vision

Transform objective testing into actionable coaching decisions that improve athletic performance.

Every measurement should lead to a meaningful action.

---

## Core Philosophy

Every test should answer four questions:

1. Measure – What happened?
2. Understand – Why did it happen?
3. Prescribe – What should we do next?
4. Improve – Did it work?

---

## Our North Star

We are not building software to measure athletes.

We are building software that helps coaches make better decisions.
